What We Do

AI Vision System Capabilities

Nexxoraa AI vision systems are built to handle the full spectrum of industrial inspection challenges — from micro-defect detection to high-speed dimensional verification.

Defect Detection

Detect surface cracks, chips, scratches, contamination, and dimensional anomalies at production speed with AI-driven precision.

Presence / Absence Inspection

Verify that every component, label, fastener, or part is present and correctly positioned before the product moves downstream.

Surface Inspection

Full-surface analysis to identify texture deviations, colour inconsistencies, coating failures, and microscopic surface defects.

Dimensional & Pattern Verification

Sub-millimetre measurement of component dimensions, hole positions, thread profiles, and geometric tolerances at line speed.

Quality Validation

End-to-end quality gate that generates real-time OK/NOK decisions, logs inspection data, and supports quality reporting.

Line Automation Support

Seamless integration with your existing PLCs, conveyors, and rejection mechanisms for fully automated, hands-free quality control.
